A trip down memory lane - how it all started...
We are Svetlana and Vesna, two friends whose paths initially crossed in 2006 where a shared passion for all things fashion, languages, travel and interiors bonded us. Both native of Serbia, little did we know that we would move to the UAE few years later, let alone start Quaint Candles.
Quaint, a homegrown Dubai-based concept launched in 2021, is inspired by simplicity and sustainability; this luxory candle concept embodies the spirit of modern minimalist design. Hand-poured with love, the Quaint candle collection has been assembled as a must-have array of designs that will add a romantic touch and a sense of warmth to any space.
Made from a natural soy wax blend, these sculptural beauties are certainly not your ordinary candles. We find great inspiration in seeing our candles as a part of your home decor. Oftentimes, Quaint Candles are brought as gifts and knowing that we are your choice when it comes to picking the thoughtful gifts for your loved ones, evokes the feeling of happiness and gratitude. Writing those special cards, celebrating the important dates and milestones big and small make our hearts skip a bit each time.
