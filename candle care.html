If you’ve noticed rough, bumpy tops after burning a soy wax candle, that’s just soy wax showing off its character. Also, that’s how you can be sure that you’re burning a naturally-derived product. Why we choose all-natural soy wax? It’s made from environmentally friendly, renewable resources. Candles made with soy wax burn clean with no soot or smoke. It is an excellent fragrance carrier that doesn’t require chemical amplifiers. It burns slower than other candle waxes.
