Not your ordinary candles

Quaint is a female-owned homegrown business in Dubai launched in 2021. Inspired by simplicity and sustainability, our luxury candle concept embodies the spirit of modern minimalist design. We choose our ingredients carefully and make our candles with love. Quaint candles are made from 100% natural soy wax. Each candle is hand poured with love and care in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
Made from a natural soy wax blend, these sculptural beauties are certainly not your ordinary candles. We find great inspiration in seeing our candles as a part of your home decor. Oftentimes, Quaint Candles are brought as gifts and knowing that we are your choice when it comes to picking the thoughtful gifts for your loved ones, evokes the feeling of happiness and gratitude.
